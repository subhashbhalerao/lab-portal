About Lab Logbook

  1. Student must do entry in the Lab Log book register
  2. Student must do all entries correctly in the logbook, i.e. sn, system name, sap id, student name and sign
  3. Student must forword the logbook to the next student after completing his entry
  4. Students must complete the logbook entries in the first hour of lab time
  5. Student must ensure of doing entry in logbook before leaving the lab
  6. It's students whole responsibilty to do entry in lab logbook

About handling computers

  1. Student must start the computer only after instructed by the faculty
  2. Student must shutdown the computer properly before leaving the lab
  3. Student must switch off the power buttons after shutting down the computer
  4. Student must arrange the chair before leaving the lab
  5. Student must handle the computers carefully
  6. Student must not move or bend computer angle and position
  7. Student must not put any thing on the computer table except pen, pencil and notebook
  8. Student must keep their bags at one place in the lab instucted by the faculty
  9. Student must keep water bottle down the computer table
  10. The computer only have four connections, usb keyboard, usb mouse, power adapter and network port
  11. Student must get familiar with computer connections
  12. If computer is not starting then check for computer connections, else inform the lab assistant or faculty
  13. If student find anything unusual with the computers, immediately inform to the lab assistant or faculy

About Lab cleanliness

  1. Student must not bring eatables in the lab
  2. Student must not make the lab untidy by throwing paper, pen, pencil etc in the lab
